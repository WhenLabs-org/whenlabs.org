* Note

WhenLabs is the studio name for everything I make. It's not a company and it isn't raising — it's a room where small, precise things get built and written about. Sometimes that's a CLI. Sometimes it's a paper. Occasionally it's a strange half-thing that doesn't have a category yet.

The bet is that the most useful work right now sits at the seam between software and research on how machines talk — so the studio keeps both on the same shelf. If any of it is useful to you, good. If you want to write, siddharth@whenlabs.org.
